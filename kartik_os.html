Detailed Recipes
Overnight Oats Parfait — Your Recipe
Ingredients (1 serving)
½ cup rolled oats · ¾ cup whole milk · 80g fresh paneer (blended) · 2 tbsp fresh cream · 2 tbsp chia seeds · 6–8 strawberries · pinch of cinnamon
Method
Add oats to a glass/bowl. Pour milk over. Stir well. This is your base layer.
Blend paneer + fresh cream until smooth. Add cinnamon. Pour as second layer.
Sprinkle chia seeds as third layer.
Blend strawberries (no sugar). Pour as top layer.
Cover and refrigerate overnight (minimum 6 hours).
Morning: eat straight from the fridge. Zero cooking needed.
Spinach Omelette — Detailed
Ingredients
3 whole eggs · 1 cup fresh spinach (chopped) · 1 small potato (boiled, diced) · 30g cheese (grated) · salt, black pepper, cumin, turmeric · 1 tsp ghee/olive oil
Method
Boil potato the night before. Refrigerate. Saves 15 mins in morning.
Beat 3 eggs with salt, pepper, turmeric, cumin.
Heat pan on medium. Add ghee. Sauté spinach for 1 minute.
Add diced potato. Toss for 30 seconds.
Pour egg mixture over. Let set for 2 mins. Flip.
Add cheese on top. Fold. Serve immediately.
Marinated Paneer — Night Prep
Marinade (prepare previous night)
200g paneer (cubed) · 3 tbsp hung curd · ½ tsp turmeric · 1 tsp red chilli · 1 tsp jeera · ½ tsp garam masala · salt · 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste · squeeze of lemon
Method
Mix all marinade ingredients. Coat paneer cubes fully.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Morning: heat pan/air fryer to high.
Air fry at 200°C for 8 mins OR pan sear 2 mins per side.
Serve with sliced cucumber + mint leaves. No bread needed.
